Job Application Policies

Welcome to the Job Application Portal - Policies

Please read the following rules and regulations carefully before applying for any job.

General Job Application Rules

  • All applicants must provide accurate and truthful information in their job applications.
  • Misrepresentation of qualifications or experience can lead to disqualification.
  • All submitted applications are subject to review and verification by the hiring company.
  • Applicants are encouraged to submit applications only for positions that match their skill set.
  • Repeated submissions of the same application for different roles in the same company are discouraged.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must meet the minimum qualification and experience requirements outlined in the job listing.
  • Applicants must not have any active criminal record or be under any legal restrictions that prevent employment.
  • Age requirements, if applicable, must be adhered to.
  • Some roles may require specific certifications or training. Applicants should ensure they meet these before applying.

Conduct During the Application Process

  • Applicants are expected to behave professionally throughout the application process.
  • Communication with the hiring company must be conducted in a respectful and formal manner.
  • Failure to attend scheduled interviews or assessments without prior notice may lead to disqualification.
  • Applicants should refrain from harassing hiring managers or HR personnel for updates.
